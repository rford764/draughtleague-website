How it works

Fantasy sports meets
your Friday night

Four simple steps to turn your brewery trips into friendly competition.

01

Join a league

Browse public leagues or get invited by a friend. Brewery, winery, or distillery — pick your poison.

02

Check in

Arrive at a venue, open the app, and check in. Answer a few questions about your visit to earn points.

03

Earn points

Score big for first visits, photo uploads, drink selections, and bonus questions. Every outing counts.

04

Climb the board

Watch the leaderboard update in real time. Seasons end with a winner — then it all resets and begins again.

League types

Choose your
category

Three distinct league types, each with their own scoring, photo tags and culture.

🍺
Brewery League

Track tap rooms, craft breweries and beer halls. Score points for tap lists, pints, tours and more.

🍷
Winery League

Explore vineyards, tasting rooms and wine bars. Earn points for tastings, bottles and cellar visits.

🥃
Distillery League

Whiskey, gin, rum and beyond. Score for flights, tours and discovering new small-batch spirits.

Features

Everything you need
to play

🏆

Live leaderboard

See exactly where you stand at all times. Points update the moment you check in — no waiting until the end of the week.

📸

Photo scoring

Earn bonus points for uploading photos of your visit — tap lists, taprooms, tours and more. Document your journey.

🗓️

Seasons

Leagues run in 3, 6 or 12-month seasons. When the season ends, the leaderboard resets and a new champion is crowned.

🔒

Private & public leagues

Run a public league anyone can join, or keep it private with invite codes and passwords for your crew.

🔔

Venue updates

Get notified when venues you've visited post new tap lists, events and promotions. Stay in the loop.

📍

Discover nearby

Traveling? Find venues other league members have visited near you, sorted by distance. Never miss a spot.
